MLR-X 1.0 — Multiple Linear Regression without limits

Desktop tool to fit and diagnose multiple linear regression models on small or large datasets, with optimized routines and interactive visualization support.

Why choose MLR-X?

Scalable and efficient

Process small or large datasets with parallel execution and lazy loading of heavy libraries.

GUI and CLI modes

Intuitive graphical interface for fast exploration or a command line for reproducible pipelines.

Reports and diagnostics

Includes validations, standard metrics (MAE, MSE, R²), and outputs ready for export.

Open source

Distributed under AGPL-3.0; contribute, review the code, and build extensions on an open base.
